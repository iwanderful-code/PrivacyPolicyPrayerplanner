Prayer Planner App

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 8, 2026

Your privacy is of paramount importance to us. This Privacy Policy governs the Prayer Planner mobile application (the "App") and explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our services.

1. Information Collection and Use

To provide a fully-synchronized experience across devices, enable community prayer sharing, and generate tailored devotional materials, the App may collect the following types of data:

  • Authentication & Profile Data: If you sign in via Google Sign-In or other supported authentication methods, we collect your basic profile details, including your name, email address, and profile picture, to uniquely secure your account.
  • User-Generated Content: We securely store content you explicitly create or interact with, such as your daily planner lists, prayer requests, journal entries, and comments left on our shared community board.
  • Selected Preferences & Focus Moods: When utilizing the scripture generator feature, we process the specific topics or moods (e.g., peace, guidance, gratitude) you select to request tailored devotionals on your behalf.

2. Use of AI Technologies (Gemini API)

The App utilizes the Google Gemini API to generate personal scripture reflections and devotional content.

  • We do not share your personal identifiers (such as email, real name, or location) with the Gemini API.
  • Only your selected abstract focus words or mood tags (such as "hope" or "anxiety") are transmitted to the API to generate appropriate scripture references and reflections.

3. Third-Party Services and Cloud Infrastructure

We use verified, secure industry-standard cloud providers to operate the App's storage and core functions. These include:

  • Google Play Services: Required to support Android features, application delivery, and basic telemetry.
  • Firebase Authentication & Cloud Firestore: Used for secure user login, database persistence, real-time community boards, and profile management.

These third-party platforms handle your data in accordance with their respective security policies. You can consult the Google Privacy Policy for more information on their practices.

4. Data Retention & Deletion Rights

We believe you should have full control over your personal data:

  • Your data is kept for as long as your account remains active.
  • You may delete any of your specific planner lists, prayer requests, or community posts at any time directly inside the App.
  • If you wish to fully delete your account and clear all associated records from our Firebase servers, you can initiate this request by contacting us directly at iwanderful@gmail.com.

5. Security of Your Data

We implement industry-accepted encryption and access controls to secure your profile and cloud records. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet or cloud storage solution is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not designed or intended to solicit or collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will immediately delete this from our database.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any modifications by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this document.
